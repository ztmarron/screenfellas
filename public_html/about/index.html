About ScreenFellas

ScreenFellas Entertainment is a multimedia company that focuses on reviewing and recapping television and movies. Starting out as a podcast hosted by Carlos and Ozzy, produced by Enrique, ScreenFellas has tripled their team. Now including Bob as Head of marketing, Myles as Head of Video Production, Eli as Director of Music, Jesse as Creative Consultant, and Zack as Technical Director the company has expanded to Youtube, with reactions of trailers, behind the scene footage, and broadcasting live.
